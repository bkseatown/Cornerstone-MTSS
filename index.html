Quick Start
  1. Pick a focus (CVC, digraphs, vowel teams, etc.).
  2. Play Word Quest together (whole class or small group).
  3. Apply skills in Story Fill, Read & Think, or Speed Sprint.
Tip: Use Teacher Settings inside Word Quest to manage audio, assessments, and classroom tools.

Teacher Notes

This platform is designed to be simple to run: predictable steps, clear visuals, and features that support small groups, intervention blocks, and whole-class projection.
